Core Capabilities

Engineered from the ground up to keep your financial graph strictly on your personal device.

🤖

Native SMS Auto-Parsing

On Android, our Kotlin SmsReceiver.kt intercepts transactional SMS broadcasts in real-time. It extracts merchants, amounts, and payment sources locally using pattern regex without ever sending your messages to a remote server.

Android Exclusive Kotlin MethodChannel
📦

Drift SQLite WebAssembly

The web target utilizes compiled sqlite3.wasm alongside dedicated web workers to bring native-grade relational database queries and reactive stream subscriptions directly into the browser's IndexedDB sandbox.

Web & Desktop Drift 2.34+
👻

Ghost Bills Prediction Engine

Proprietary local statistical analysis identifies recurring subscription patterns across your transaction history, predicting upcoming liabilities and reserving budget headroom before charges hit your account.

Local Intelligence Zero Analytics
